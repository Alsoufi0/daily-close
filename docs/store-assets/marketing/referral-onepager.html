Daily CloseRefer-a-Friend

Owners earn free store-months for bringing in other owners.

A simple two-sided program: an existing owner shares a link, a new owner signs up and pays, and both win — the newcomer gets a welcome deal, the referrer gets free store-months that match how big a customer they brought in.

🎉
When Owner B makes their first payment, Owner A earns one free store-month for every store B paid for (about $50 each) — applied as credit on A’s next bill. Only that first payment counts.

The reward follows what the newcomer brings, not how big the referrer already is — so a 10-store owner who refers a 1-store shop earns ~$50, not ~$499. And because it only pays out on stores B actually paid for, it can’t be gamed with fake sign-ups.

Three quick decisions
1
“Pick which stores” — build it, or skip it?
All stores cost the same and bill as one lump, so “5 free store-months” is just a $250 credit no matter which stores it’s pointed at. A store-picker screen is purely cosmetic.
Recommendation: skip the picker — just apply the credit
2
If the reward is bigger than the referrer’s bill, what happens?
e.g. a 2-store owner ($100/mo) earns $250. The extra $150 can carry over and reduce the following months automatically.
Recommendation: let it roll over to future months
3
Cap how much one owner can earn?
Since every reward is backed by real money the newcomer paid, there’s little fraud risk. A hard cap isn’t really needed.
Recommendation: no hard cap — just a quiet alert on large payouts
This is separate from the existing partner/distributor program (outside salespeople who earn cash commissions). It also rides on the same discount & credit system — so it’s one build, not two.
